professional development

One crucial realization I had as of lately was on the topic of feedback. We had discussed the topic in one of our PD sessions and I realized the importance of not only being receptive to feedback, but also seeking feedback from others. It plays a key role in not only professional growth, but personal growth as well. By asking for feedback, I am able to identify areas of improvement and work towards becoming a better version of myself. This mindset shift has allowed me to embrace feedback as a tool for learning, polishing my skills, and ultimately becoming a stronger collaborator.

technical development

With technical development, I have realized the importance of anchoring in all that is taught. It is easy to just memorize how codes are supposed to be written, but I have a passion to understand the full inner workings of why codes do the things they do. By fully understanding the concepts, I am able to build a strong foundation that allows me to adapt and learn new technologies more easily in the future. This mindset has helped me become a more confident and passionate developer, as I am not just relying on memorization, but truly understanding the concepts behind the code.

personal development

Within my personal development, I have realized that I am more than capable of overcoming any difficult topics. Instead of getting frustrated and giving up when things get difficult, I am able to lead, motivate, and support myself throughout the process. I have learned to break down complex problems into smaller, manageable tasks, and to approach challenges with a positive mindset. This has allowed me to build resilience and confidence in my abilities, and to see difficult topics as opportunities for growth and learning.
